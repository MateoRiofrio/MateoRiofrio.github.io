About me:

I was born and grew up in Quito, Ecuador. Currently, I live in Vermont, where I have lived for the past eight years. I pursued a bachelor's degree in computer science at the University of Vermont and graduated in the spring semester of 2021. Recently, I have been interested in app development, which is why I based my first app on one of my hobbies: biking. I have used it to to track a 40-mile ride around the islands of Vermont. Other than that, I am passionate about rock climbing and hope to begin climbing outdoors soon.
